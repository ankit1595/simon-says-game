How to Play
  1. Guess the WORDLE in six tries.
  2. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.
  3. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  4. If the tile turns green then the letter is in the word and in the correct spot
  5. If the tile turns orange then the letter is in the word and in the wrong spot
  6. If the title turns grey then the letter not in the word in any spot.
